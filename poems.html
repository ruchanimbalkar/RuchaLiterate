A best friend and shoes

A best friend who keeps your shoes safe for months so you can have them when you get back from an overseas trip.

A best friend who offers to buy you many shoes  to stop you from going back to your abusive boyfriend.

A best friend who insists that you buy new shoes instead of crying over a prick.

A best friend like that is a godsend.

I thank all those

I wrote this response especially for my loved ones and family. I am grateful to all my friends, family , acquaintances and even strangers for helping me, being kind to me. I believe I am still here and growing because of the unconditional love I have received from this people. It is just the goodness of God for putting so many awesome people in the World.
