A best friend and shoes

A best friend who keeps your shoes safe for months so you can have them when you get back from an overseas trip.

A best friend who offers to buy you many shoes  to stop you from going back to your abusive boyfriend.

A best friend who insists that you buy new shoes instead of crying over a prick.

A best friend like that is a godsend.

I thank all those
Poem by Rucha Nimbalkar

I thank all those who believed in my dreams,
You have helped me beat self-doubt
I thank all who told me to be true to myself;
They have taught me the value of inner peace

I thank all who told my faults to my face
You have shown me how much you cared
I thank all those who never gave up on me;
You have demonstrated to me the goodness of God
I thank all those who saw the best in me;
when I had lost everything and had suicidal thoughts

I thank all those who loved me so much,
They gave me the strength to move on from toxic relationships
I thank all those who have treated me with utmost dignity and respect;
You have shown me what I deserve
I thank all those prayed for me,
They have taught me the power of prayer

Most importantly, I thank all who love me for no reason
They are the blessing in my life

"SUPERHEROES OF INDIA" is a collection of poems on Indian national heroes by Rucha Nimbalkar. This superhumans went out of their way to help fellow countrymen. They built this country and enlightened others.

A man who was committed to the betterment of society
he was sincere and cared deeply

A savior who sacrificed everything
unfortunately was shot one morning

A social activist full of empathy
went out of his way to end brutality

His fight was against superstition and ignorance
blinded by fear; between wrong and right, some could not see the difference

The villains were self-proclaimed God men
who took unfair advantage of anybody's faith

He was not afraid of death
he enlightened others and did his best

The murderers who got him killed thought they won
but we won't let it happen

We will not be fooled by con -men and thugs
we won't follow stupid traditions and sacrifice animals

This superhero's work will continue forever
A law to ban superstitions is put in order
