About Rucha Literate

Hi, there. My name is Rucha Nimbalkar. I write poems to express myself or maybe they write through me. Writing has helped me heal.It gives me joy and I have fun doing it. In addition to writing, I also read. Fiction, Spiritual/ Self-Help are my favorite major genres. Moreover, in fiction I like mysteries, fairytales and suspense books. I like to talk about books and discuss on YouTube.
